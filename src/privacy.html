Privacy Policy

Introduction

This privacy policy (“Privacy Policy”) applies to all visitors and users of the moatmt.chat hosted services and websites (collectively, the “Website” or “Websites”), which are offered by Moatmt Chat Company for AI and E-commerce Automation Solutions and/or any of its affiliates (“Moatmt Chat,” “we,” “us,” or “our”). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using any part of the Websites, you acknowledge that you have been informed of and consent to our practices regarding your personal information and data.

Data Protection

Oversight of data security is handled by Moatmt Chat’s respective Data Protection Officers. If you wish to request modifications, deletions, or additions to any personal data you believe is captured by Moatmt Chat, or if you have any general security concerns, please contact us at: privacy@moatmt.sa

What Information Moatmt Chat Collects and Why

Information from Website Visitors: Like most website operators, Moatmt Chat collects basic non-personally identifying information from Website visitors (browser type, language preference, referring page, date/time of request). This is used to improve the platform, enhance experience, and safeguard the Website. We may publicly release aggregated non-identifiable data (e.g., reports on usage trends). We also collect IP addresses for performance, content optimization, and security monitoring. We do not use this to personally identify users.

Behavioral Statistics: Moatmt Chat may gather usage-based statistical data to improve services (e.g., download volumes, feature adoption) using anonymized analytics.

Personally-Identifying Information

You may choose to provide personally identifiable information while interacting with Moatmt Chat (e.g., via live chat, account creation, support inquiries). In such cases:

  • A User ID may be generated for tracking system performance.
  • Live chat requires full name and email.
  • Account creation stores user data securely.

If you report a security vulnerability and request acknowledgement, your provided name may be published to fulfill your request. We only collect such data as reasonably required to fulfill your request. Users may choose not to share this data but may lose access to some features.

Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

Moatmt Chat does not intentionally collect sensitive personal data such as national ID numbers, health, genetic, or religious information. If you intentionally submit sensitive information via chat or API, you consent to its storage on our servers (hosted securely in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia). Users under age 13 may not create accounts. If discovered, accounts will be terminated. If your country has different minimums, local regulations apply.

Basis for Processing Your Information

  • Performance of Contract – Required to deliver Moatmt Chat services.
  • Legitimate Interests – Improving products, preventing fraud, ensuring security.
  • Consent – Used for direct marketing communications only (e.g., newsletters), which users can opt out from anytime using unsubscribe or contacting privacy@moatmt.sa.

How We Use and Protect Information

Sharing Your Information: Moatmt Chat only discloses personally identifiable information to employees or contractors who require it for processing, and to authorized service providers who have agreed not to disclose it to others. User email and IP addresses may be shared with their deployment administrators. Moatmt Chat does not rent or sell user information. Disclosure is permitted if legally required or to protect Moatmt Chat, the public, or third-party rights. Logos of client companies using our software may be displayed unless explicitly objected to via email to info@moatmt.sa.

International Transfer of Information

The Website and servers are located in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Data may also be securely processed by our teams in other regions under international compliance standards. By using our Website, you consent to such transfers under Moatmt Chat management.

Moatmt Chat Communications

If you are registered and provided your email: we may contact you about system updates, security, features, feedback, or product news. Marketing emails contain an unsubscribe link. Support or feedback requests may be published in anonymized formats to help other users—excluding personal data.

Cookies & Tracking

Cookies: Moatmt Chat uses cookies to identify users, track sessions, and improve platform usage. You may disable cookies in your browser, but certain features may not function correctly. Some third-party cookies may appear (e.g., for embedded video content). We do not control third-party cookies.

Tracking Technologies: Third-party analytics tools may be used to gather non-identifiable technical performance data only.

Do Not Track

Moatmt Chat does not track users across external websites and does not adjust based on Do Not Track browser settings.

Global Privacy Practices

For all users worldwide, Moatmt Chat ensures transparent consent processes, data minimization, access/correction/deletion options, accountability, protection, legal processing limits, and right to dispute or escalate concerns. Users in the EU are granted additional rights. To exercise privacy rights: privacy@moatmt.sa.

Data Retention and Deletion

You may update or delete your profile through account settings. Moatmt Chat retains data while account is active, as needed to fulfill services or legal obligations, and to resolve disputes & enforce agreements. Content shared in community forums or support archives may remain for operational integrity (non-personal identifiers only).

Contacting Moatmt Chat About Your Privacy

If you have any concerns or wish to exercise your rights, email us: privacy@moatmt.sa. Please use subject line: “Privacy Concern”. Response time: within 30 days maximum.

Privacy Policy Changes

Moatmt Chat may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will post updates on our Website and may notify registered users in case of material updates. Continued use of our platform signifies acceptance of the revised privacy policy.
